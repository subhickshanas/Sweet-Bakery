Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Do you offer custom orders?

Yes! We offer custom orders for weddings, birthday parties, and special occasions. Please contact us at least 2 weeks in advance.

❓ What are your delivery options?

We offer local delivery within 10 miles. Standard delivery takes 1-2 business days. Rush orders available on weekends.

❓ Do you have allergen information?

Yes! All our products are labeled with allergen information. Please contact us for detailed allergy concerns.

❓ Can I place orders online?

Absolutely! You can browse and order all our products through our website or contact us directly for custom orders.

❓ What's your return policy?

We guarantee fresh products. If you're unsatisfied, contact us within 24 hours for a full refund or replacement.

❓ Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only deliver locally. However, we're exploring international shipping options. Contact us for more info!
